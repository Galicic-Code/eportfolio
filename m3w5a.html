Week 5 Artefact: Week 5 Activity

After reading the article by Barafort et al (2018) and the blog by Kirvan (2021), the following questions were answered:

    1. 1. Which of the frameworks do you think would be applicable to the following organisations:


    2. a. International bank

      3. Bank would need to comply with the ISO 27001 standard due to its Information Security Management systems (ISMS) framework. Their issues are regulatory compliance, personal confidential information and financial transactions.

    3. b. Large hospital

      4. Hospitals deals with personal confidential information and financial transactions (especially if they are a private, rather than a public hospital). They would need to apply the HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) in the USA and compliance with the Data Protection Act (DPA) 2018 & the Common Law Duty of Confidentiality, if they are based in the UK.

    4. c. Large food manufacturing factory


      5. Besides applying Occupational Health & Safety standards, such as ISO 45001:2018 (Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems), the best frameworks for food manufacturing factories are the ISO 22000 (Food Safety Management) and the ISO 9001 (Quality Management System)
