Week 10 Activity

After reading Opara-Martins et al (2014) and Morrow et al. (2021), the following questions were answered:

1. What are some of the main vendor lock-in issues the authors identify? How would you mitigate them?

a) System incompatibility
b) Lack of interoperability with other (cloud) applications
c) Exclusive licencing
d) Limited data migration (portability)
e) lack of standards / standardisation
f) Data integration, e.g. APIs
g) Cost
h) Hardware limitations

2. What are some of the security concerns with the modern cloud? How can these be mitigated?

a) Legal - different juristictions
b) Legal - legal disputes
c) Legal - policies
d) Access rights (unauthorised access)
e) Data - encryption
f) Data - removal/storage
g) Data - back-ups
h) Incident response

References

Morrow, T., LaPiana, V., Faatz, D., Hueca, A. & Richmond, N. (2021) Cloud Security Best Practices Derived from Mission Thread Analysis. Carnegie-Mellon Univ Pittsburgh PA.

Opara-Martins, J., Sahandi, R., & Tian, F. (2014) Critical review of vendor lock-in and its impact on adoption of cloud computing. In International Conference on Information Society (i-Society 2014) (pp. 92-97). IEEE.
